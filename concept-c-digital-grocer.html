// thesis

No fluff. Just flavor, indexed.

Gemrin is a working grocery, shipped as a website. Every SKU you see is on the shelf right now. If it's sold out, we mark it out. If it's in, you can walk in and grab it.

We don't do stories. We don't do slideshows. We do rice, we do sauce, we do vegetables that were in a field yesterday.
